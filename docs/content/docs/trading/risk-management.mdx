FoxBlaze intercepts all trade requests and evaluates them against our internal `RiskService` before executing them on-chain.

## Limits and Constraints
Before any `OPEN_POSITION` job is sent to the BullMQ execution queue, your trade validates against two constraint systems:
1. **Sufficient Margin**: Verifies you have enough collateral to open the position.
2. **Leverage Limit**: Validates that your requested multiplier does not exceed the asset's specific `maxLeverage` allowed by Hyperliquid.

## Take Profit & Stop Loss

You can configure TP and SL directly during the Interactive Setup Panel before executing the trade. 

### Post-Trade Configuration
If the position is already open, you can dynamically modify the TP/SL limits using Telegram commands:

- **Take Profit**: `/tp <ticker> <price>` (E.g. `/tp SOL 180`)
- **Stop Loss**: `/sl <ticker> <price>` (E.g. `/sl SOL 120`)

These commands will enqueue a `SET_TP_SL` background task which safely modifies or adds reduce-only trigger orders on the exchange.

<Callout title="Execution Speed" type="info">
TP and SL orders sit natively on the Hyperliquid orderbook. The FoxBlaze agent does not soft-evaluate prices locally via WebSockets, ensuring absolute minimal latency and preventing slippage during high-volatility events.
</Callout>
