Monitoring your active trades is seamless with FoxBlaze's visual rendering engine.

## Command: `/positions`

Typing `/positions` will fetch your live open trades. Rather than text, the bot generates a dynamic, high-fidelity **PNL Visual Card**. This card contains:

- Entry Price vs Mark Price
- Leverage and Asset Size
- Current TP/SL Parameters
- Unrealized PnL (in USD)
- Return on Equity (ROE%)
- The last 12-hour 15-minute price sparkline for the asset

## Closing Positions

There are two ways to close an open position:
1. **Interactive Button**: Beneath the PNL Card, click the `✖️ CLOSE` inline button for the specific asset.
2. **Command**:
   - `/close <ticker>`: E.g., `/close BTC` to market-close the specific position.
   - `/close ALL`: Liquidate *all* active positions simultaneously via concurrent RPC calls.

<Callout title="After Closing" type="success">
When a position successfully closes, FoxBlaze will render your **Realized PNL Card** showing your final profit and ROE. You can easily share this natively to your Telegram channels.
</Callout>
