---

## View your positions

Type `/positions` to see all your active trades. The bot generates a visual **PnL Card** showing:

- Asset name and direction (Long/Short)
- Entry price vs current price
- Leverage and position size
- Unrealized profit/loss (USD)
- Return on equity (ROE%)

## Close a position

You have two options:

1. **Tap the button**: Below the PnL Card, tap `✖️ CLOSE` next to the position you want to close.
2. **Use a command**:
   - `/close BTC` — Close your BTC position
   - `/close all` — Close everything at once

<Callout title="PnL Card" type="success">
When a position closes, FoxBlaze generates a beautiful **Realized PnL Card** showing your final profit. Share it directly to your Telegram channels!
</Callout>

## Other useful commands

| Command | What it does |
|---|---|
| `/balance` | View your total equity and available margin |
| `/orders` | See all pending limit and trigger orders |
| `/history` | View recent closed trades |
