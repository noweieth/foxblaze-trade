After setting up your wallet, you need to deposit funds. FoxBlaze operates directly on Hyperliquid L1, meaning all deposits go through the native bridge.

<DepositDiagram />

## Deposit Address

1. **Copy your Wallet Address**: Use the `/start` command to get your wallet address.
2. **Send USDC**: Send native USDC (not USDC.e) on the **Arbitrum One** network to your wallet address. Minimum deposit is 10 USDC.
3. **Automatic Scanning**: The FoxBlaze bot continuously monitors your address via the `DepositService` scanning the Arbitrum RPC.

Once the balance reaches > 1.0 USDC, the `AUTO_DEPOSIT` background queue is triggered. Your USDC will be gaslessly bridged to Hyperliquid.

<Callout title="Why Arbitrum?" type="info">
Hyperliquid's native L1 bridge operates via the Arbitrum network, allowing low fee and fast bridge transactions directly to the Perps exchange without requiring an EVM gas token.
</Callout>
