To start trading, you need to deposit USDC into your FoxBlaze wallet.

## How to deposit

1. **Get your address**: Type `/deposit` or `/start` in the bot to see your wallet address.
2. **Send USDC**: From any wallet (MetaMask, Coinbase, Binance, etc.), send **USDC on Arbitrum One** to your address.
3. **Tap the button**: After sending, tap **"🔄 I have completed my deposit"** in the bot. It will scan and detect your funds automatically.
4. **Auto-bridge**: Once detected, your USDC is bridged to Hyperliquid with **zero gas fees** — no ETH needed.

## Important notes

- **Network**: Only send USDC on **Arbitrum One**. Other networks (Ethereum, BSC, etc.) are not supported.
- **Minimum**: First deposit must be at least **10 USDC** to activate margin trading.
- **Speed**: Bridging usually takes under 30 seconds after detection.

<Callout title="Why Arbitrum?" type="info">
Hyperliquid uses Arbitrum as its native bridge network. This means low fees and fast transfers — no ETH gas required.
</Callout>
