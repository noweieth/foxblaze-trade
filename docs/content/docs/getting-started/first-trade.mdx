Once your wallet is funded, you're ready to trade. Here's how:

## Open a position

1. Type `/long` to go long, or `/short` to go short.
2. Enter the asset ticker (e.g. `BTC`, `ETH`, `SOL`).
3. An **Order Setup Panel** appears with all your trade parameters.

```text
🦊 FOXBLAZE ORDER SETUP 🦊

🪙 Asset: BTC
📊 Side: LONG
💰 Margin: $10
🚀 Leverage: 10x
🎯 TP Price: None
🛡️ SL Price: None
```

## Adjust your trade

Use the inline buttons to customize:

- **Size** — How much USDC you want to put in ($10, $25, $50, $100, or custom)
- **Leverage** — Multiplier for your position (5x, 10x, 20x, or custom)
- **Take Profit** — Auto-close when price hits your target
- **Stop Loss** — Auto-close to limit your loss

## Execute

Tap **Confirm Trade** when ready. The bot verifies your margin, checks leverage limits, and places a market order on Hyperliquid instantly.

<Callout title="Quick tip" type="info">
You can also open trades directly from the chart view! Tap the Long/Short buttons below any chart.
</Callout>
