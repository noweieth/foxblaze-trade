Once your wallet is funded, you are ready to open your first position.

<TradeDiagram />

## The Trade Command

1. Send `/long` or `/short` to the bot.
2. The bot will prompt you for an asset ticker (e.g. `BTC`, `SOL`).
3. An **Interactive Order Setup Panel** will appear.

```text
🦊 FOXBLAZE ORDER SETUP 🦊

🪙 Asset: BTC
📊 Side: LONG
💰 Margin: $10
🚀 Leverage: 10x
🎯 TP Price: None
🛡️ SL Price: None
```

## Setup Panel Configuration

You can adjust the parameters using the inline keyboard.

- **Size**: Adjust your position collateral.
- **Leverage**: Change the leverage multiplier (capped at the asset's max leverage).
- **Take Profit / Stop Loss**: Set auto-exit triggers. 

When you click a custom button, the bot will ask you to reply with your desired value via Telegram's Force Reply modal.

## Confirmation

Once ready, click **Confirm Trade**. The background `TradeService` will verify your risk limits and place a market order directly on the Hyperliquid exchange.
