---
title: Create a Wallet
description: How to generate your non-custodial trading wallet.
---

To begin, start a conversation with the FoxBlaze Telegram bot and type `/start`.

## The Onboarding Flow

1. **Initialization**: The bot will recognize your Telegram ID and generate a secure **HD Wallet (BIP-39/44)** on the Arbitrum network.
2. **Private Keys**: Your private keys are generated securely and encrypted with AES-256 before being stored in the database. FoxBlaze operates entirely non-custodially via the Hyperliquid Agent Key structure. We never have access to withdraw your funds.
3. **Wallet Address**: The bot will reply with your new deposit address.

```text
✅ Registration Successful!

💳 Your Trading Wallet (Arbitrum)
0x1234abcd5678...
```

<Callout title="Note" type="info">
To unlock Margin Trading, a minimum first deposit of 10 USDC is required on the Arbitrum network. This is due to Hyperliquid's minimum deposit restriction for Agent API approval.
</Callout>
