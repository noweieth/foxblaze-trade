title: Create a Wallet
description: How to create your trading wallet in seconds.
---

Open the FoxBlaze bot on Telegram and type `/start`. That's it — your wallet is created automatically.

## What happens when you type /start?

1. **Wallet created**: The bot instantly generates a secure trading wallet on Arbitrum for you.
2. **Address displayed**: You'll see your unique deposit address right in the chat.
3. **Ready to fund**: Copy the address and send USDC to start trading.

```text
✅ Registration Successful!

💳 Your Trading Wallet (Arbitrum)
0x1234abcd5678...
```

## Quick actions

After setup, you'll see buttons to:
- **📥 Deposit USDC** — Fund your account
- **📊 Balance** — Check your funds
- **📈 Chart BTC** — View live charts
- **❓ Help** — See all commands

<Callout title="First deposit" type="info">
A minimum deposit of 10 USDC (Arbitrum network) is required to activate margin trading on Hyperliquid.
</Callout>
